Crokinole (WIP)

A digital twist on the classic tabletop game Crokinole, blending skill and strategy. Players flick discs aiming for high scores by landing in scoring zones or knocking opponents’ discs off the board — similar in spirit to curling but fast-paced and finger-powered. Built in Unity with smooth physics and intuitive controls.

VR Curriculum Based Application

An educational VR experience built in Unity that demonstrates the law of reflection. Players use mirrors to guide a beam of light toward a target while avoiding obstacles—designed to teach physics concepts through interactive, immersive gameplay.
